Help

  • Pages to print

    You can mention page numbers or page ranges, comma separated.

    Examples

    • 1 will print just the first page.
    • 1,4 will print the first and the fourth page.
    • 1-3 will print pages 1 through 3.
    • 1,3-5,7 will print pages 1, 3 through 5 and page 7.

  • Pages per sheet

    You can enter 1, 2, 4, 6, 9 or 16 to arrange that many document pages into a grid on one single sheet of paper e.g for printing powerpoint slides.

  • The printer ran out of paper

    Once you load it with paper, don't press print above again! It will result in two printouts. Instead, press the OK button on the printer.
